QULALITY POLICY

Sreenivasa Institute of Technology and Management Studies strives to establish a system of quality assurance to continuously address, monitor and evaluate the quality of education offered to students, thus promoting effective teaching processes for the benefit of students and making the college a Centre of Excellence for Engineering and Technological studies.
